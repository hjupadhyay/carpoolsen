Frequently Asked Questions


Q1. Carpooling. Why should I?

Well, for starters, there is the benefit of saving fuel. Plus, a surprising amount of people using personal vehicles to travel to a particular destination are open to the idea of sharing rides with acquaintances. A carpool might be the start of a good friendship!

Q2. I don't trust strangers. What then?

Keeping obvious security concerns in mind, we have segregated users into different categories. Users who provide some sort of an authentication (like a driving licence) are classed as "authenticated" users. In the misfortunate circumstance that something goes wrong, we have all the details needed to get them!

Q3. What about unauthenticated users?

Unauthenticated users are solely the risk of the user hitching a ride. We make our best efforts to keep our system clean, but cannot make any guarantees about them. We definitely insist that all users registering authenticate themselves ASAP!

Q4. Are there separate accounts for posting and reserving rides?

No. Any user can be a poster of rides or be reserving rides at any given time. There are no separate accounts.

Q5. Is there a fee to use this system?

Not an outright fee as such. This website is mainly supported by advertisements. Also, we charge a small percentage from whatever the users earn from the people sharing their vehicle. This helps us pay for servers and coffee that keeps us awake when we work on improving the website!

Q6. Is there a cost involved with hitching a ride?

Users offering you a ride may or may not ask for a nominal fee. We suggest recommended prices to every user posting a ride based on the length of the trip and other parameters like vehicle quality (Air-Conditioned or not etc). It is up to the poster to follow these recommendations or not. Some may not charge anything at all. We as such don't charge anything from someone just making a reservation for a trip.

Q7. What are negative flags?

Negative flags are system generated negative ratings given to a user when s/he does an action which is inconvenient to fellow users. These actions are mainly: 1) If a user cancels his/her post after there is already one or more reserved users. 2) If a user edits his/her post after there is already one or more reserved users.
